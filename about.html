Meet Our Founder

Jose Martinez

Founder & Lead Contractor

Founded in 2024 by Jose Martinez, Vista Roofing has quickly established itself as a premier roofing contractor serving St. George and surrounding areas.

With years of experience in the roofing industry, Jose brings a commitment to excellence and attention to detail that sets Vista Roofing apart. Our company specializes in both residential and commercial roofing projects, offering comprehensive solutions for all your roofing needs.

Based in St. George, Utah, we understand the unique challenges that Southern Utah's climate presents to roofing systems. From the intense desert sun to occasional severe weather, we know what it takes to keep your property protected.
